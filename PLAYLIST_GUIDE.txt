RECOMMENDED SONGS BY EMOTION:
------------------------------

HAPPY FOLDER (Cheerful, upbeat songs):
- "Happy" by Pharrell Williams
- "Walking on Sunshine" by Katrina & The Waves
- "Can't Stop the Feeling" by Justin Timberlake
- "Good Vibrations" by The Beach Boys
- Any upbeat pop, dance, or feel-good songs

SAD FOLDER (Melancholic, emotional songs):
- "Someone Like You" by Adele
- "Fix You" by Coldplay
- "Hurt" by Johnny Cash
- Slow, emotional ballads
- Note: In "Change Mood" mode, these won't play when you're sad!

ENERGETIC FOLDER (High-energy, fast-paced):
- "Eye of the Tiger" by Survivor
- "Thunder" by Imagine Dragons
- "We Will Rock You" by Queen
- Rock, EDM, workout songs
- Fast tempo, high energy

CALM FOLDER (Relaxing, peaceful):
- "Weightless" by Marconi Union
- "Clair de Lune" by Debussy
- Nature sounds, meditation music
- Slow, peaceful, ambient music


IMPORTANT NOTES:
----------------
1. The app will automatically detect songs when you restart it
2. You can add as many songs as you want to each folder
3. The app randomly selects a song from the appropriate folder
4. Songs loop until your emotion changes
5. If a folder is empty, no music will play for that emotion


QUICK SETUP:
------------
1. Create folders (if they don't exist):
   - music\Happy
   - music\Sad
   - music\Energetic
   - music\Calm

2. Add at least 1-2 songs to the "Happy" folder (most important for mood-changing!)

3. Restart the app

4. The app will automatically load all songs from the folders
